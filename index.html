    Self/Full Serve

    Decide whether to make product and technology investments in increasing ease-of-use, or in adding new and more powerful functionality

    Select the amount & type of investment by sliding the scale
    • Easy to Use
    • Powerful

    "Ease of Use" means a focus on making features quick and easy to setup while incorporate the mobile expertise and best practices offered by Vibes. This would be accomplished by offering "tried and true" campaign flows including built in compliance language. As a result, the opportunity for setup error and possibility of having non-compliant campaigns would be minimized. This will also make it harder to customize campaigns to specific user flows or to match how a customer had their campaign setup at their last vendor.

    "Powerful" means a focus on more customizable features that would rely more on the expertise of the user to achieve success. The platform would more of a collection of features that the user could combine to create the the desired mobile experience. Compliance and best practices would be offered as suggestions through the platform or advised from Vibes mobile experts. This will also make it easier for the user to "shoot themselves in the foot" as the flexibility will allow more errors in setup or missing compliance language.
